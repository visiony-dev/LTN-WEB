About Live & Notify Tour

Our Story

Founded in 2025, Live & Notify Tour began with a passion for Tanzania’s wild landscapes and a vision to offer unparalleled safari experiences. Starting with small group tours in Serengeti, we’ve grown into a trusted leader in luxury safari adventures, guiding thousands of travelers to the heart of Tarangire, Ngorongoro Crater, Mikumi, and beyond.

Our Mission & Values

Our mission is to deliver unforgettable, premium safari experiences that connect travelers with Tanzania’s stunning wildlife and rich culture. We value sustainability, expert guidance, luxury, and creating lasting memories for every guest.

Our Expert Team

Our team includes seasoned safari guides with over 1 years of experience, expert naturalists certified by Tanzania National Parks, and dedicated travel coordinators committed to crafting personalized itineraries. Meet our lead guide, James Mwangi, a Serengeti expert with a passion for sharing the Great Migration with the world.

What Sets Us Apart

We offer private, luxury safaris with expert guides, gourmet dining under the stars, and sustainable practices that protect Tanzania’s natural beauty for future generations. Our partnerships with premier lodges and conservation organizations ensure an unmatched premium experience.
